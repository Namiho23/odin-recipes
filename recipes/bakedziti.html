Baked Ziti

A delicious main dish

Have baked ziti for dinner tonight, it's a great family dinner with beef,tomato sauce and a lot of cheese.

Great with a salad and garlic bread on the side

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 oz. package dry baked ziti
  • 1 lb lean ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 28 oz jars spaghetti sauce
  • 6 oz provolone cheese
  • 6 oz mozzarella cheese
  • 1 1/2 cups sour cream
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped basil

Steps

  1. Step 1: Great with a salad and garlic bread on the side
  2. Step 2: In a large skillet, brown beef over medium heat. Add onions; saute until tender. Drain off fat and add spaghetti sauce; simmer for about 15 minutes.
  3. Step 3: Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  4. Step 4: In a lightly greased 2 quart baking dish, place about half of the pasta; top with a layer of provolone and mozzarella cheese slices. Spread on a layer of half the spaghetti sauce mixture and sour cream.
  5. Step 5: Cover with remaining pasta, cheese and sauce; sprinkle a layer of Parmesan cheese and fresh basil.
  6. Step 6: Bake in preheated oven for about 30 minutes or until cheese and sauce are bubbly; serve.
