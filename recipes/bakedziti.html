Baked Ziti

A delicious main dish

Have baked ziti for dinner tonight, it's a great family dinner with beef,tomato sauce and a lot of cheese.

Great with a salad and garlic bread on the side>

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 oz. package dry baked ziti
  • 1 lb lean ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 28 oz jars spaghetti sauce
  • 6 oz provolone cheese
  • 6 oz mozzarella cheese
  • 1 1/2 cups sour cream
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped basil
